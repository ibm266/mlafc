Meet your consultant

Professor Dhiraj Gupta

MB BS, MD, DM, FRCP (London)

Professor Gupta has been a consultant at Liverpool Heart and Chest Hospital — the United Kingdom's largest specialist cardiothoracic centre — since 2007. He is honorary Professor of Cardiology at the University of Liverpool, Senior Lecturer at Imperial College London, and a medical advisor to the AF Association, the UK's national charity for arrhythmia patients.

He has earned national repute in the UK for his expertise in treating atrial fibrillation, and proctors cardiologists across the United Kingdom, United States and Europe in complex procedures. Consultations available in English, Hindi and Punjabi.

How the Mumbai clinic works

London expertise. Mumbai care. Continuous follow-up.

Professor Gupta visits Mumbai for in-person consultations and procedures during scheduled clinic dates. Between visits, your care is coordinated with trusted local cardiologists — so you are never without a doctor close to home.

i. Consultation

An unhurried first visit

Meet Professor Gupta in person during his scheduled Mumbai clinics. He reviews your history, tests and symptoms in detail, and explains your options clearly — in English, Hindi or Punjabi.

ii. Treatment

Procedure in Mumbai

If a procedure is needed — catheter ablation, LAA occlusion, pacemaker or device — it is performed in Mumbai with our partner hospital teams, to the same protocol Professor Gupta uses in the UK.

iii. Follow-up

Continuity, locally

Your ongoing care is managed by trusted Mumbai cardiologists with Professor Gupta supervising remotely. You get specialist oversight without flying anywhere for routine follow-up.

Conditions & treatments

What we look after.

The clinic specialises in disorders of heart rhythm — when the heart beats too fast, too slow, or irregularly — and the procedures used to correct them.

Conditions we treat

  • Atrial fibrillation (AF)
  • Atrial flutter
  • Supraventricular tachycardia (SVT)
  • Palpitations & unexplained heart racing
  • Unexplained blackouts & dizziness
  • Bradycardia (slow heart rhythms)

Procedures offered

  • Catheter ablation for atrial fibrillation
  • Ablation for SVT and other arrhythmias
  • Left atrial appendage occlusion (LAAO)
  • Pacemaker implantation
  • Implantable defibrillators (ICDs)
  • Cardiac resynchronisation therapy
