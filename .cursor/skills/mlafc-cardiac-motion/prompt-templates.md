# Prompt templates

## af, Atrial fibrillation

**Still:** Schematic four-chamber heart outline. NO text, labels, or titles.
Upper chambers (atria) filled with chaotic squiggly lines in ink-mute. Lower
ventricles calm. Bottom ECG strip with irregularly spaced brass peaks. Dotted
brass arc above heart. 16:9 aspect ratio.

**Motion:** Fixed camera, no zoom, no pan. Atrial squiggles quiver continuously
at irregular speed, never organising. ECG line scrolls with peaks at uneven
intervals. Gold dot on arc pulses at irregular timing. No text added. Loop-friendly.

## flutter, Atrial flutter

**Still:** NO text, labels, or titles. Distinct from AF: smooth regular sawtooth
zigzag lines in both atria (not chaotic squiggles). Circular brass arrow loop
in right atrium showing re-entry circuit. Bottom ECG: regular fast sawtooth
F-waves with evenly spaced QRS spikes. 16:9.

**Motion:** Fixed camera, no zoom, no pan. Sawtooth lines scroll steadily
left-to-right in perfect rhythm. Gold dot races around circular loop at steady
speed. ECG sawtooth and spikes march left at regular even intervals. No text.
Loop-friendly.

## svt, Supraventricular tachycardia

**Still:** Heart with a simple circular re-entry loop arrow in brass connecting
atrium to ventricle and back. ECG strip: narrow complex tachycardia, regular fast.

**Motion:** Brass dot travels the re-entry loop continuously. ECG peaks march
left-to-right at steady fast rate.
