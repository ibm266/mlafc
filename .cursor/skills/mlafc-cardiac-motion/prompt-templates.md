# Prompt templates

## af, Atrial fibrillation

**Still:** Schematic four-chamber heart outline. Upper chambers (atria) filled
with short chaotic squiggly lines in ink-mute. Lower chambers calm. Bottom ECG
strip shows irregularly irregular rhythm in brass on ink baseline. Small label
callouts: "Atria, disorganised" and "Ventricles, irregularly filled".

**Motion:** Chaotic atrial squiggles gently flicker, then partially organise.
Brass ECG dot travels along dotted arc above heart. Bottom ECG strip pulses
irregularly. Barely perceptible heart outline breathe (scale 1–2%).

## flutter, Atrial flutter

**Still:** Heart schematic with sawtooth pattern in atria (ink-soft). ECG strip
shows flutter waves (regular sawtooth) in brass.

**Motion:** Sawtooth pattern scrolls smoothly left-to-right in atria. ECG
sawtooth animates in sync. Slow push-in.

## svt, Supraventricular tachycardia

**Still:** Heart with a simple circular re-entry loop arrow in brass connecting
atrium to ventricle and back. ECG strip: narrow complex tachycardia, regular fast.

**Motion:** Brass dot travels the re-entry loop continuously. ECG peaks march
left-to-right at steady fast rate.
